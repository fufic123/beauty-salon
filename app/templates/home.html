Who is Lisa Franklin and what inspired the brand?

Lisa Franklin is a respected authority in skincare—a product developer, nutritionist, and founder of our London-based clinic and multi-award-winning skincare line. With a career spanning many years, Lisa created the brand to reflect her belief that skin health is intrinsically linked to overall wellbeing. What began as a vision to blend clinical expertise with personalised care has evolved into a destination for advanced, regenerative treatments and science-led skincare. Today, Lisa Franklin London is proud to be a certified B Corporation—recognised for its commitment to ethical practices, sustainability, and purposeful innovation in beauty and wellness.

Why choose Lisa Franklin London for skin care?

At Lisa Franklin London, we take a holistic approach to skin health—treating the skin as a reflection of your overall wellbeing. Rooted in the philosophy of Clinic Privé, our approach blends advanced aesthetic technology, regenerative medicine, ophthalmology, and time-honoured techniques to deliver truly personalised care. From medical dermatology and eye health support to hormonal balance, cellular rejuvenation, and bespoke facials, every treatment is designed to restore radiance, clarity, and long-term skin vitality.

What makes Lisa Franklin skincare products different?

Each Lisa Franklin formula is created with precision—blending biotechnology, potent actives, and nature’s intelligence to deliver visible, skin-enhancing results. Designed to extend our clinic expertise into your daily routine, every product supports skin health, radiance, and long-term resilience. As a certified B Corporation, we are committed to ethical innovation, sustainable practices, and performance without compromise. Our award-winning range combines scientific efficacy with luxurious textures—crafted in the UK to deliver transformative care from clinic to home.
